embroidery

Embroidery was my hobby for a long time, besides from the 2008 till 2014 it was my official work - I embroidered a lot of scene costumes for famouse Ukrainian and Russian artists, as well as I worked with design studio LUVI. From the 2013 I started to work on my documentary and to learn a film process and almost never embroidered at that period because didn't feel this form of art to explore myself. But over time, I realized that this kind of art is just as important and interesting for me as cinema. At last 3 years I am gradually returning to it, created a few new works and have a big plans at this field of art.
